# Echo Beta Week 01 Plan

## Purpose

This is the practical operating plan for the first week of the controlled Echo beta.

It turns the beta from a vague intention into a daily sequence.

## Day 0: Final Check

Before inviting real people:

- confirm the opening feed still looks alive
- confirm the reserve catalog is ready
- confirm creator setup and upload still work
- confirm the admin is showing reports and moderation correctly
- confirm the issue log and daily review template are ready

## Day 1: Small Opening

Open with a very small group.

Target:

- `3 to 5` creators
- `5 to 10` listeners

Watch:

- did they understand what Echo is quickly?
- did anyone save without being told twice?
- did creators get through setup and upload without rescue?
- did each creator persona show a usable week-one pulse?

## Creator Week 01 Pulse

For each creator persona, read only:

- `Abertura`
- `Folga ativa`
- `Publicadas`
- `Reserva`

This is enough for the first week.

Do not inflate week one with a heavy dashboard.

## Operator Use

Treat the pulse as a go/no-go read:

- weak abertura: hold onboarding
- no second upload: still early
- zero folga ativa: cleanup before next push
- reserve too large too early: review clutter and release choice

## Day 2: First Adjustment

Review:

- strongest opening tracks
- weakest opening tracks
- confusion in creator setup
- reports or trust friction

Act:

- swap weak opening tracks if needed
- improve wording where confusion repeated
- log every real complaint

## Day 3: Controlled Expansion

If Day 1 and Day 2 were stable:

- invite the next small group
- widen the listener set slightly

Do not expand yet if:

- the feed still feels weak
- creators are still asking how to publish
- moderation already feels noisy

## Day 4 And Day 5: Pattern Reading

Look for:

- first saves without prompting
- first follows that feel intentional
- creators returning to publish again
- repeated weak patterns in the feed

The goal here is not volume.

The goal is to see whether the product is becoming more alive or only busier.

## Day 6: Operator Review

Write down:

- what is working
- what feels fragile
- what still makes Echo feel like a prototype
- what already feels like product

## Day 7: Week 1 Decision

Choose one:

- widen the beta
- hold the beta and repair weak points
- rerun another small week with a tighter cohort

## Week 1 Success Signs

- listeners save a few tracks naturally
- creators publish without rescue
- at least one creator wants to come back and post again
- the feed feels stronger at the end of the week than at the start

## Week 1 Failure Signs

- people do not understand the point of Echo quickly
- creators stall in setup or upload
- the catalog still feels fake or thin
- moderation already feels messy
- the operator cannot explain clearly what changed during the week
