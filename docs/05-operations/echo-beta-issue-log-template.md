# Echo Beta Issue Log Template

## Purpose

This template gives the Echo beta a single structure for issue logging.

Every meaningful issue should be captured in the same shape.

## Issue Fields

- date
- area
- severity
- summary
- evidence
- current impact
- proposed action
- owner
- status

## Area Labels

- feed quality
- upload friction
- creator confusion
- moderation load
- reporting failure
- trust / spam risk
- catalog weakness
- analytics mismatch

## Severity Labels

- low
- medium
- high
- critical

## Table Template

| Date | Area | Severity | Summary | Evidence | Current Impact | Proposed Action | Owner | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| YYYY-MM-DD | feed quality | medium | First screen feels repetitive | 3 weak tracks in top 10 | weaker first-session value | rotate reserve catalog | operator | open |

## Status Labels

- open
- observing
- fixing
- resolved
- deferred
