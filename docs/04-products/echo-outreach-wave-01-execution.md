# Echo Outreach Wave 01 Execution

## Purpose

This file turns the first outreach wave into a simple execution order.

It should be used when the founder is ready to start contacting real creators.

## Wave 01 Order

### 1. DARKWAVE FREQUENCIES

- priority: `A`
- reason to open here first:
  - strongest identity match for Echo's darker lane
  - clear public work
  - likely to understand discovery-first positioning

Suggested opening:

```text
Hey, I came across DARKWAVE FREQUENCIES and liked how clear the cinematic identity already feels.

I'm building Echo, a small beta focused on helping AI music creators get discovered early, before they disappear into generic platforms.

We're inviting a first wave of creators with strong identity and room to grow, and your work looks like a strong fit for that.

If you're open, I'd love to invite you to test it with one track first and tell us what feels weak or confusing.
```

### 2. AGV Music

- priority: `A`
- reason to open here second:
  - repeated releases
  - reflective creator voice
  - likely better-than-average feedback quality

Suggested opening:

```text
Hey, I found AGV Music while looking for AI music creators who are clearly taking the work seriously, not just uploading random tracks.

I'm building Echo, an early music platform focused on discovery-first for AI creators.

What made me reach out is that your work already feels intentional, and you also seem like the kind of creator who could give honest feedback on what still feels weak.

If you're open to a small controlled beta, I'd love to share the steps.
```

### 3. Dizzo AI Music

- priority: `B`
- use if:
  - one of the A candidates does not respond
  - the first wave needs broader fit testing

Suggested opening:

```text
Hey, I saw your AI music channel while mapping early creators for a small Echo beta.

Echo is being built as a place where AI music creators can actually be discovered early instead of getting buried.

We're inviting a small first group to test setup, upload and first-track visibility. If you're interested, I can send the beta path.
```

### 4. Zero Yard Range

- priority: `B`
- use if:

  - the team wants to test non-electronic fit earlier

Suggested opening:

```text
Hey, I found Zero Yard Range while looking for smaller AI music creators with a clearer lane than generic feed-filling content.

I'm building Echo, an early platform focused on discovery-first for AI music creators, and the alt-rock angle makes your profile especially useful for the kind of beta we want to run.

If you're interested, I'd be glad to send the simple beta steps.
```

## Execution Rule

After a message is actually sent:

1. change the creator from `candidate` to `invited`
2. add the send date in the notes column
3. if there is no answer after a reasonable wait, use the follow-up template

After a positive reply:

1. change the creator from `invited` to `accepted`
2. do not mark `onboarded` yet
3. only move to `onboarded` after the intake bar is complete inside the product

## Practical Reminder

The first wave is not a growth campaign.

It is a learning wave.

That means:

- better small and responsive than big and silent
- better a creator with honest friction than a bigger creator with no interest
