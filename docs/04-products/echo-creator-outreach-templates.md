# Echo Creator Outreach Templates

## Purpose

These templates exist so outreach for the first Echo beta cohort stays consistent.

They should be adapted lightly, not sent like cold automation.

## Short DM

```text
Hey, I'm building Echo, a small beta focused on helping AI music creators get discovered without the usual friction.

We're inviting a first wave of creators whose work already shows clear identity, and your profile looks like a strong fit.

If you're open to testing it, the ask is simple:
- set up your creator profile
- upload one track
- upload a second if the flow feels clear
- tell us what feels confusing or weak
```

## Slightly Longer Invite

```text
I'm building Echo, an early platform for AI music creators where the promise is visibility first, not instant payout.

We're running a controlled beta with a small creator group to learn what actually helps creators feel seen and what still creates friction.

Why I reached out:
- your work already has a recognizable identity
- you seem like someone who would give honest feedback
- you look like a better fit for discovery testing than for a mass-launch environment

If you're interested, I can send the beta steps. The goal is simple: create a profile, upload a track, and tell us what feels off.
```

## Follow-Up

```text
Following up in case this got buried.

We're still shaping the first Echo creator wave and I think your profile is still a strong fit.

No pressure if it's not the right moment, but if you want early access and a chance to shape the product while it's still small, I can send the beta details.
```

## Acceptance Reply

```text
Great. Here's the beta path:

1. create or confirm your creator profile
2. upload one track
3. if the first upload feels clear, upload a second
4. tell us where the flow is confusing, slow, or unfair

This is a controlled beta, so the most useful thing you can give us is honest friction, not polished praise.
```
