# Echo External Cohort Wave 01

## Purpose

This document defines the first external creator wave for the Echo controlled beta.

It exists so external onboarding starts with intention, not improvisation.

## Wave 01 Goal

Bring in the first creators who are external to the project but still close enough to the product thesis to give useful feedback.

Wave 01 is not about scale.

Wave 01 is about:

- validating creator comprehension
- validating upload clarity
- testing whether the visibility-first promise resonates
- learning where creator onboarding still breaks

## Wave Shape

- primary target creators: `6`
- reserve target creators: `4`
- first invite window: `3 to 5 days`
- expected accepted creators: `3 to 5`
- expected onboarded creators in first pass: `2 to 4`

## Selection Rule

Wave 01 should favor creators who are:

- already making AI music publicly
- still small enough to care about discovery
- likely to post more than once
- able to explain what confused them

Do not optimize this wave for follower count.

Optimize it for learning quality.

| W1-01 | melodic electronic micro-creator | TikTok / short-form | tests broad accessibility and identity fit | candidate |
| W1-02 | lo-fi or chill loop creator | Discord / community | tests repeat-listen behavior and save intent | candidate |
| W1-03 | dark atmospheric AI producer | Reddit / community | tests niche mood lane and creator clarity | candidate |

| W1-R3 | experimental but coherent creator | Discord / Reddit | tests edge-of-feed tolerance without chaos | reserve |
| W1-R4 | fast iterative hobbyist | any lane | tests whether repeat uploads happen naturally | reserve |

## Acceptance Bar

Invite only if the candidate appears to match at least `4` of these:

- clear creator identity
- understandable track naming
- more than one public post or release
- no obvious spam pattern
- no dependence on immediate monetization
- some sign of caring about discovery or audience reaction

## Wave 01 Success Signal

Wave 01 is successful if:

- at least `3` creators accept
- at least `2` complete setup without rescue
- at least `2` upload a second track
- at least `1` gives useful product feedback without prompting

## Wave 01 Failure Signal

Wave 01 fails if:

- most invites are ignored because the positioning is unclear
- creators need too much manual rescue
- creators do not understand what Echo promises
- creators upload once and disappear immediately

## Operator Note

Do not treat silence from Wave 01 as only a sourcing problem.

If multiple candidates ignore or decline the same way, the positioning or onboarding is probably still weak.
