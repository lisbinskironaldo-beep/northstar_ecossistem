# Echo Creator Onboarding Playbook

## Purpose

This playbook defines how the first creator cohort should be onboarded into Echo during the controlled beta.

It is intentionally lightweight.

The goal is not scale yet.

The goal is to learn where creators struggle and what makes them return.

## Creator Cohort Goal

The first creator cohort should test:

- creator setup
- upload clarity
- category choice
- metadata quality
- emotional response to the platform

## Beta Cohort Size

- initial creators: `10 to 25`
- reserve creators: `5 to 10`

## Ideal Creator Profile

Prioritize creators who are:

- already experimenting with AI music
- small enough to care about discovery
- motivated by visibility, not immediate payout
- able to publish more than one track during beta

Avoid prioritizing:

- spam-heavy uploaders
- users who only want instant monetization
- creators with obviously inconsistent identity

## Onboarding Message Structure

Every first creator invite should communicate:

- what Echo is
- why they were invited
- what exactly they should do
- what kind of feedback matters most
- that this is a controlled beta, not a finished launch

## Creator Tasks During Beta

Ask each creator to:

1. create or confirm a creator profile
2. upload at least one track
3. upload a second track if the first flow was clear
4. describe any confusing field or step
5. report if they feel the app undervalues or misrepresents their work

## What To Observe

During onboarding, capture:

- where they hesitate
- what fields confuse them
- whether category choice feels obvious
- whether they understand the promise of Echo
- whether they would return without being asked

## Beta Creator Success Signals

- creators complete setup without rescue
- creators upload at least one track successfully
- some creators voluntarily upload again
- creators understand the visibility-first positioning

## Beta Creator Failure Signals

- creators need operator help to finish setup
- metadata fields are misunderstood
- creators do not understand category selection
- creators leave after a single forced upload

## Operator Rule

Do not over-explain the platform during onboarding.

If too much explanation is needed, the product is still too confusing.
