## The Real Secret

The trick is not only better recommendation.

The real product edge is:

- giving the user strong taste control
- making libraries feel alive and social
- keeping the surface simple while the system underneath does the heavy work

Echo should feel like:

- a discovery app
- a taste app
- a collector app

Not only a streaming app.
