# Echo Mobile Premium Artist Home

## Purpose

This document defines the stronger artist surface now taking shape inside Echo mobile.

The artist home should feel like:

- a mini-world
- a music identity
- a social destination

It should not feel like:

- a raw profile page
- a dashboard panel
- a noisy social network

## Current Rule

The mobile feed is the entry.

The artist home is the destination.

That means:

- release cards stay short and social
- a tap outside the card actions opens the artist home
- the fixed player selection is preserved while the listener explores the artist

## Current First Premium Pass

The artist home now includes:

- a stronger hero area
- artist handle and identity
- lightweight signal strip
- featured release area
- slim release list
- lightweight reaction actions
- live/show timeline
- live-radio placeholder surface

## Product Logic

### Feed role

The feed answers:

- who is launching now
- what is worth opening

### Artist-home role

The artist home answers:

- what kind of artist is this
- what should I hear first
- what else has this artist released
- what is coming next

That split keeps the home feed lighter.

## Social Rule

The artist home should feel social through music actions:

- follow
- like
- emoji
- comment
- save playlist
- borrowed album

Not through image-heavy profile clutter.

## Live Rule

The artist home is also where scheduled live activity becomes legible:

- upcoming live radio
- upcoming show
- upcoming release window

This is where the listener learns that the artist is active,
without turning Echo into a heavy live-video system.

## Ecosystem Rule

The artist home now sits inside a subtle ecosystem horizon.

For listeners:

- Echo is the live music front
- Pulse and Lumen stay visible only as horizon hints

For creators:

- Echo is active now
- Pulse and Lumen are visible as future workspace expansion

That keeps ambition visible without making the current app feel unfinished.

## Why This Block Matters

Without a stronger artist home:

- the feed stays shallow
- discovery feels disposable
- the artist has no real stage

With it:

- the feed can stay lighter
- discovery feels intentional
- artists feel more worth following
